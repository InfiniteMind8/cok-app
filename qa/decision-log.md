| D-001 | 2026-04-28 | A.1 — No email template engine exists in Phase 1 | Use `@react-email/render` + `@react-email/components` for server-side HTML rendering of email templates | (a) Plain HTML strings in .ts files — maintainable but fragile; (b) Handlebars/Mustache — extra dep with no React integration | New dependencies: `@react-email/render`, `@react-email/components`.
