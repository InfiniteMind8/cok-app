## 1. Who can use Karis

The City of Karis Community App ("Karis", "the app") is a private platform for the residents, vendors, master administrators, and visitors of the City of Karis development in Guyana. Access is by invitation only, granted at the discretion of the City of Karis administration.

By using Karis you confirm that you are:

- at least 18 years of age, or
- a legal dependent of an authorised primary tenant who has accepted these terms on your behalf.

You agree to provide accurate registration information and to keep your contact details up to date.

## 2. Your account

- One account per person. Sharing credentials is prohibited.
- Multi-factor authentication is required for staff roles (Master Admin, Admin) and recommended for all other members.
- You are responsible for activity that takes place under your account except where we determine, in consultation with you, that the account was compromised through no fault of your own.
- Lost credentials are recovered through the procedure in the Profile screen and, where MFA reset is required, through the Master Admin.

## 3. Acceptable use

You will not:

- Misrepresent your identity, role, or property linkage.
- Use Karis to harass, threaten, defame, or discriminate against any person.
- Attempt to access data or features beyond your role.
- Probe, scan, or test the security of the platform without prior written authorisation from the project owner.
- Upload content (including images) to which you do not hold the necessary rights.
- Submit false information in onboarding, voting, or lease lifecycle workflows.

The City of Karis may suspend or terminate access for any violation, with or without notice, in accordance with the community's governance.

## 4. KCRD, Promotions Wallet, and conversions

- The City of Karis Community Rewards Dollar (KCRD) is an internal community accounting unit. It is **not** legal tender, **not** a security, and **not** a means of payment outside the Karis community.
- KCRD balances and ledger entries are operational records of the community. They confer no claim against the City of Karis Limited, its officers, or its assets, except as expressly granted in writing through the City of Karis governance.
- Conversions between KCRD and fiat currencies (USD, GYD) are processed using the rate published in the app at the time of the conversion. Once a conversion is recorded on the ledger it is final.
- Promotional bonuses paid out of the Promotions Wallet are at the discretion of the project owner and may be revised or revoked at any time, subject to the community's governance.
- Treasury floors, system-wallet limits, and approval thresholds are set by the Master Admin.

## 5. Lease lifecycle

If you are a Resident or Tenant:

- Your lease status (Active, Ending Soon, Expired) is calculated from the start date and term recorded by the Master Admin.
- Extension requests are subject to Master Admin approval. The community is under no obligation to extend any tenancy.
- Where rental cycles roll over automatically, payment due dates are derived from the lease record. Disputed rollovers must be raised with the Master Admin within 30 days.

## 6. Visitor groups

Visitor accounts have time-bound access to the announcements and timelines associated with the groups they have been added to. Visitor access can be revoked at any time by the Master Admin without notice.

## 7. Communications

By using Karis you agree to receive operational emails (sign-in confirmations, MFA challenges, lease alerts, emergency broadcasts, treasury reconciliation alerts) at the email on your account. These are **not** marketing communications and you cannot opt out of them while your account is active.

## 8. Service availability

We aim for high availability but do not guarantee uninterrupted access. Planned maintenance is announced in-app where practical. Force-majeure events, upstream provider outages, and security responses may require unscheduled downtime.

## 9. Termination

You may close your account at any time by contacting a Master Admin. The City of Karis may suspend or close any account for violation of these terms or where required by law. Certain data must be retained as described in the Privacy Policy.

## 10. Limitation of liability

To the maximum extent permitted by law, the City of Karis Limited and its operators are not liable for indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages, lost profits, or lost data, arising out of your use of the app. Direct damages, where they apply, are limited to the value of the KCRD or fiat balance recorded against your account at the time of the incident.

## 11. Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of Guyana. Disputes that cannot be resolved through the City of Karis community governance will be submitted to the courts of Georgetown, Guyana.

## 12. Changes

We will notify members in-app and by email at least 14 days before any material change to these terms.
