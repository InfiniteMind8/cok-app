## Full description (≤4000 chars)

```
City of Karis is the private community application for residents, vendors, master administrators, and authorised visitors of the City of Karis development in Guyana. Access is by invitation; the app is not open to the general public.

Inside the app, members manage everything that comes with living and working in a thoughtfully designed community.

For Residents and Tenants
— View your KCRD wallet balance, transactions, and conversion history. Display amounts in KCRD, USD, or GYD.
— File issues for the Master Admin team and track them to resolution.
— Request rental extensions and follow lease milestones (Active, Ending Soon, Expired).
— Vote on community proposals and read announcements relevant to your role and visitor groups.
— Verify identity, manage 2FA, and keep your contact details current.

For Master Admins
— Approve property transfers, voucher requests, and rental extensions from a single Approvals Center.
— Run the treasury — system wallets, fee schedules, conversion rates, promotion bonuses, and reconciliation alerts.
— Bulk-import members and properties from spreadsheet templates.
— Send emergency broadcasts to the entire community or to specific visitor groups.
— Audit every privileged action and export a member's complete data record on request.
— Configure visitor groups for time-bound access to selected announcements.

For Vendors
— List offers and manage redemptions through the integrated voucher and payment flow.
— Receive transactional notifications and track activity in your dashboard.

For Visitors
— Tap into the announcements and timelines for the groups you have been invited to.
— Time-bound access; revocable at any time by the Master Admin.

Security and trust
— Multi-factor authentication for staff roles.
— Encrypted file storage for identity documents and property attachments.
— Detailed audit trail of every administrative action.
— Reconciliation alerts that catch ledger mismatches early.
— PII-scrubbed error monitoring.

Payments inside the app are accounted for in KCRD, the City of Karis Community Rewards Dollar, an internal community accounting unit. KCRD is not legal tender and is not a means of payment outside the Karis community.

The City of Karis. Beautiful, empowered living in Guyana.
```
